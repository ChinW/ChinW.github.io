  • 替换homebrew默认源

    12月 28, 2016 in Ruby

    来源https://lug.ustc.edu.cn/wiki/mirrors/help/brew.git

    $ gem sources –add https://gems.ruby-china.org/ –remove https://rubygems.org/
    $ gem sources -l
    https://gems.ruby-china.org

    确保只有 gems.ruby-china.org
